Hi! My name is Joshua Bullock.

About Me:

Welcome to my portfolio website! First a couple things about me, I am a 20ish graphic designer/coder. I grew up in Pittsburgh, PA I like long walks on the beach and I have experience with Javascript, c#, Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop and the P5.js library.

About the website:

To create the website you are currently on I used HTML5 and CSS(no premade templates were used). I also mostly used flexbox to position things on the page instead of CSS grid. The largest challenge for me was figuring out how to display information about each of the items in my portfolio. Deciding whether to somehow display all of that information on the same page as the original image or to take it to a separate page was a decision that would greatly change the layout of my portfolio page. Something that I'm proud of on this website but isn't necessarily min is the transitioning colors on the home page the code was written by u/OverZealousCreations on Reddit and it creates a CSS animation that has the affected area transition into different colors of the rainbow at regular intervals.
