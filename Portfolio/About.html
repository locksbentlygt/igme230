Hi! My name is Joshua Bullock.

About Me:

Welcome to my portfolio website! First a couple things about me, I am a 20ish graphic designer/coder. I grew up in Pittsburgh, PA I like long walks on the beach and I have experience with Javascript, c#, Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop and the P5.js library.

About the website:

To create the website you are currently on I used HTML5 and CSS the largest challenge for me was figuring out how to display the information about each of the items in my portfolio. I went through a few ideas, each ranging from having the information in a drop-down menu to all of the information being behind the image and then being shown when the user clicks on the image by dropping the image down a couple hundred pixels. But due to time constraints, I had to place all of the information onto a separate page entirely. Another challenge that I didn't really expect was picking and choosing what I wanted to show in my portfolio. Displaying any of my javascript projects I feel would not have fit the design for this website and I still had to decide what designs I was proud of enough to place them in my portfolio.
